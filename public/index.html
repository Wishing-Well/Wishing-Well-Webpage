Welcome to Wellit

Crowdfunding community projects with your well wishes.

Community Projects

Support a project that needs to raise funds such as supplies for painting, fixing a raised sidewalk, or flood damage recovery, Wellit can help collect donations from the community.

Donate

Visit a project's well and toss a donation for good will.

Well Wishes

Leave a goodwill message, or a wish for the project.

Each time you donate, you'll create a ripple effect that generates goodwill towards your community.

Features

Get to know your community and be involve by visiting wells in your neighborhood. All you need is an android phone and use the GPS to find the wells.
